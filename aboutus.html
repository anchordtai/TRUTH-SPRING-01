About Apostle Olutimehin Richard

Apostle Olutimehin Richard Adelayo is the is President/ Pioneer of SPRING OF TRUTH MINISTRIES He is an Apostle of the gospel, an Evangelist, a Writer, Crusader, Counselor, Mentor, and Revivalist. He has organised crusades, revivals , and seminars in cities, villages in Nigeria, and also special Emancipation revivals in churches. He has graduated many believers in School of Mission Evangelism and Biblical principles of Deliverance. He was Kogi State SUPERFEST coordinator for 8 years of Abundant Life Gospel Church. God has used him mostly in Ondo, Akure, Owo, Lagos, Lokoja, Ayigba, Itobe Ochedamu, Suleja, Ore, Odigbo, Omifon, Owena, Bolorunduro, and regular seminars and revivald at Abuja. Through these, souls in thousands has given their lives to Christ Jesus., many of them are general overseers, and gaints in the kingdom of God

Mission Statement

We are called to disciple believers and prepare them for the end time harvest of our Lord Jesus Christ.

We are called with the mandade of Evangelism and Mission in accordance with the christian commission (Matt. 28:19)

We are called to prech the gospel to king and their subjects and prepare them for the Kingdom Of God.
